About Go Chiang Mai

Your Gateway to the Rose of the North

Discover authentic experiences and create unforgettable memories in Chiang Mai's rich cultural heritage and breathtaking landscapes

Our Story

Founded with a passion for sharing the authentic beauty of Northern Thailand, Go Chiang Mai has been your trusted companion in exploring the Rose of the North. We believe every traveler deserves to experience the genuine warmth of Lanna culture and the breathtaking landscapes that make Chiang Mai truly magical.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to bridge the gap between curious travelers and the rich tapestry of experiences that Chiang Mai offers. From ancient temples hidden in mountain valleys to bustling night markets filled with local delicacies, we curate experiences that go beyond the ordinary.

Our Mission

We are committed to creating meaningful connections between travelers and the authentic spirit of Chiang Mai

Authentic Experiences

Connect with local communities and discover hidden gems beyond typical tourist attractions. Every experience is carefully crafted to showcase the real Chiang Mai.

Sustainable Tourism

Promoting responsible travel that preserves Chiang Mai's natural beauty and cultural heritage for future generations while supporting local communities.

Local Community Support

Supporting local businesses, artisans, and guides to ensure tourism benefits everyone and creates lasting positive impact in the community.
