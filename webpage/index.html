Travel Tips

Everything you need to know for your perfect Chiang Mai adventure

Getting There

Fly directly to Chiang Mai International Airport or take an overnight train from Bangkok for a scenic journey.

Best Time to Visit

November to February offers cool, dry weather. March to May is hot, June to October is rainy season.

Getting Around

Rent a scooter, use songthaews (red trucks), or grab a tuk-tuk for short distances. Walking is great in the old city.

Budget Tips

Street food costs 30-100 THB, temple visits are often free, and local markets offer great deals on crafts.
